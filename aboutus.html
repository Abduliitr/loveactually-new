About the Company

Our Belief - Live Life with Love.


We are a startup with services for differently-abled people, we are working on a social platform for the people with disabilities to meet new friends, and enabling them to be employable by giving them training and placing them in various organizations and making them independent. LoveActually stands for live life with love. It is designed for people with disability to come together and meet new people around them. We endorse inclusiveness. This platform is built with a prophecy to aid sovereign living and social acceptance. We are working towards becoming one of the largest communities of differently-abled people. We are partnering with various institutions to impart training and corporates to open their door to help us in enabling them.
